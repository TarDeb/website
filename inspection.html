PV Inspection with Thermal Drone Technology

Our drone‑based thermal inspections deliver fast, cell‑level analysis across your entire PV array. Using high‑resolution infrared sensors, we detect hot spots, junction box failures, string outages, soiling, module mismatch, and more—all without interrupting your system’s operation.

  • Comprehensive & scalable: Inspect up to 1 MW in under 10 minutes with autonomous flight—ideal for large-scale solar farms and commercial rooftops.
  • Early fault detection: Thermal imaging reveals hidden defects (e.g. micro-cracks, PID, hot spots, diode issues) before they become visible or costly.
  • Higher safety & lower cost: No rooftop walking, no system shutdown—data is captured from the air, keeping technicians safe and maximizing uptime.
  • Standards‑compliant data: Inspections follow IEC 62446‑3 guidelines for solar thermography, ensuring reliable results for O&M, warranty, and insurance.
  • AI‑powered analysis: Visual and thermal data are processed by AI, automatically flagging anomalies and prioritizing maintenance actions.

Every inspection includes:

  • Radiometric thermal maps for hotspot detection
  • Visual (RGB) imagery for soiling, shading, and physical damage
  • Geo‑tagged anomaly reports with GPS and temperature deltas
  • Interactive dashboards for trend tracking and compliance
